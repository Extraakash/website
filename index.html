About Me

Born and raised amid the vibrant tapestry of Mumbai, my journey has been one of curiosity and creative exploration.
From an early age, I found my calling in crafting visual wonders, channelling my enthusiasm into learning the art of design and graphics through Photoshop and even dabbling in the realm of VFX. Alongside this, a fervent passion for photography emerged, driving me to capture the essence of moments frozen in time. Today, armed with this eclectic blend of skills and an unquenchable thirst for knowledge, I harness the power of Augmented Reality to not just breathe life into my wildest imaginings, but also to share my visionary odyssey, enabling others to partake in the realms of my imagination.
